    Complaint Resolution Process

    We follow a clear, structured process to ensure every complaint is handled fairly.

    1
    Acknowledgement

    We confirm receipt and assign a reference number.

    2
    Issue Review

    Our team reviews your complaint in detail.

    3
    Investigation

    We conduct a thorough investigation where required.

    4
    Resolution

    We provide a clear response and solution.

    5
    Case Closure

    We close the case once you're satisfied with the outcome.

    Service Standards

    Our commitment to you throughout the complaints process.

    Prompt acknowledgement of every complaint
    Professional and courteous communication
    Confidential handling of personal information
    Fair and impartial resolution

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions about complaints and feedback.

    We aim to resolve most complaints within 5 working days. Complex cases may take up to 15 working days. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

    If you're not satisfied with our resolution, you can escalate your complaint to the Cooperative's Dispute Resolution Committee. Details will be provided in our response.

    While we prefer contact details to follow up, we do accept anonymous complaints. However, please note that this may limit our ability to investigate and resolve your issue fully.

    All personal information is handled confidentially and in accordance with our data protection policy. Your information is only used for the purpose of addressing your complaint.
