    What is Gold Vault?

    Gold Vault is Nobles Cooperative's premium savings product for members who want to save consistently over the long term while working towards significant financial goals.

    Key Benefits

    Gold Vault is built for serious savers who are committed to building lasting wealth.

    Long-Term Wealth Building

    Designed to help you accumulate significant savings over time.

    Disciplined Savings Structure

    A structured approach that encourages consistent saving habits.

    Accessible Through Nobles Vault

    Manage your premium savings anytime, anywhere from your phone.

    Designed for Serious Savers

    Ideal for members who are committed to achieving significant financial goals.

    How It Works

    Getting started with Gold Vault is simple. Follow these five steps.

    1
    Download Nobles Vault

    Get the app from the Google Play Store.

    2
    Become a Member

    Join Nobles Cooperative with your ₦1,000 share.

    3
    Select Gold Vault

    Choose Gold Vault as your premium savings product.

    4
    Fund Your Savings

    Make consistent contributions to your Gold Vault account.

    5
    Hold Until Maturity

    Let your savings grow until the end of the 12-month tenor.
