    Group Savings

    What is Group Savings?

    Group Savings is a digital savings solution that enables members to create trusted savings groups, contribute regularly and rotate payouts among members based on agreed terms.

    Built for community-based savings. Whether you're saving with family, friends, market associations or church groups, Group Savings brings accountability and transparency to your traditional savings circles.

    Key Benefits

    Group Savings combines the power of community with the convenience of digital accountability.

    Simple Group Management

    Easily create and manage your savings groups from one place.

    Promotes Savings Discipline

    Regular contributions build consistent financial habits.

    Transparent Contribution Records

    Every contribution and payout is recorded digitally.

    Digital Accountability

    Stay accountable through the Nobles Vault app.

    Community-Based Financial Growth

    Support each other's financial goals as a group.

    How Group Savings Works

    Getting started with Group Savings is simple. Follow these five steps.

    1
    Create or Join a Group

    Start a new savings group or join an existing one.

    2
    Invite Trusted Members

    Add family, friends or association members to your group.

    3
    Agree on Terms

    Set contribution amounts and payout schedules together.

    4
    Contribute Through Nobles Vault

    Make regular contributions via the app.

    5
    Payouts Rotate Fairly

    Payouts rotate according to your agreed plan.
