Savings product

Group Savings

The official Nobles Group Savings product for members who want to explore saving with an eligible group.

Open Nobles VaultAsk staff on WhatsApp

Who it is for

Members considering a group-based savings arrangement after reviewing the approved participation and governance rules.

The problem it addresses

Informal group arrangements can become unclear when participation, records and responsibilities are not agreed in advance.

How it works

  1. 1

    Contact Nobles staff with information about the proposed group.

  2. 2

    Review the currently approved eligibility, record-keeping and participation rules.

  3. 3

    Complete the required group and member enrolment steps.

  4. 4

    Use only the approved Nobles channel for contributions and records.

Benefits

Practical benefit

Uses the official product name Group Savings.

Practical benefit

Provides a staff-assisted route for understanding participation rules.

Practical benefit

Encourages clear records and agreed responsibilities before contributions begin.

How to enrol

  1. 1

    Download or open Nobles Vault and register as directed.

  2. 2

    Ask staff to confirm every term marked for confirmation on this page.

  3. 3

    Complete assisted office enrolment where the product requires it.

Before you commit

Do not contribute until every participant understands the approved rules, responsibilities and access conditions. Group Savings must not be described as an informal rotation scheme unless an approved source explicitly establishes that structure.
