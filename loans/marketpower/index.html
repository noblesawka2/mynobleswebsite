    Business Loan

    What is MarketPower?

    MarketPower is Nobles Cooperative's business loan designed for established traders and shop owners who require structured financing to expand and strengthen their businesses.

    Key Features

    Designed for established businesses that need structured working capital to scale.

    ₦200,000 – ₦500,000

    Significant working capital to help you scale your inventory and operations.

    16 Weeks Tenor

    Structured repayment over 16 weeks with a manageable weekly schedule.

    Weekly Repayment

    Repay weekly in structured instalments that align with your business cash flow.

    Compulsory Savings

    Build your savings throughout the loan period — securing your financial future.

    Transparent Charges

    Clear processing and insurance charges — no hidden fees.

    Eligibility

    To qualify for MarketPower, you must meet the following requirements.

    Registered Nobles Cooperative member
    Established business with verifiable operations
    Successful credit assessment
    Required guarantors or approved alternative collateral
    Business verification

    How It Works

    Getting started with MarketPower is simple. Follow these five steps.

    1
    Become a Member

    Join Nobles Cooperative with your ₦1,000 lifetime share.

    2
    Submit Application

    Apply for MarketPower through the Nobles Vault app or at our Awka office.

    3
    Complete Verification

    Provide BVN, NIN, business verification and collateral documents.

    4
    Receive Approval

    If eligible, your loan is approved and disbursed to your account.

    5
    Repay Weekly

    Repay according to your schedule — structured weekly payments.

    Loan Structure

    MarketPower grows with your business. Start with Cycle 1 and progress to Cycle 2 as you build trust and repayment history.

    ₦200,000 – ₦300,000
    Cycle 1
    16 weeks repayment
    Weekly instalments
    Ideal for first-time business applicants
    Processing fee, insurance & savings apply
    ₦301,000 – ₦500,000
    Cycle 2
    16 weeks repayment
    Weekly instalments
    Available after successful Cycle 1 completion
    Processing fee, insurance & savings apply

    Processing fee, insurance and compulsory savings apply in accordance with the approved product policy. Repayment is weekly over 16 weeks.

    Required Documents

    Prepare these documents before starting your application.

    BVN and NIN
    Passport photograph
    Business verification documents
    Guarantor or approved collateral documents
    Additional documents required during appraisal

    MarketPower requires either acceptable guarantors or approved alternative collateral. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements during the application process.

    Approval typically takes 48–72 hours after all verification and business documents are submitted. We prioritise thorough assessment while ensuring timely decisions.
