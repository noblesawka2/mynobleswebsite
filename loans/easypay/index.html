    Salary-Backed Loan

    Salary-Backed Loans Made Simple.

    EasyPay Salary Loan gives eligible salary earners access to responsible financing with structured monthly repayments designed to support personal and family needs.

    Apply for EasyPay Become a Member
    Salary Loan

    What is EasyPay Salary Loan?

    EasyPay is a salary-backed loan designed for employed members with regular income, providing responsible access to finance through an affordable monthly repayment structure.

    Designed for Salary Earners

    EasyPay is tailored for employed members with verifiable income. The loan structure aligns with your monthly salary cycle, making repayments predictable and manageable.

    Key Features

    EasyPay is built for salary earners who need responsible access to credit.

    6 Months Tenor

    Structured repayment over 6 months with a manageable monthly schedule.

    Monthly Repayment

    Repay monthly in structured instalments that align with your salary cycle.

    Designed for Salary Earners

    Tailored for employed members with regular, verifiable income.

    Transparent Pricing

    Clear charges in line with the approved product policy — no hidden fees.

    Responsible Lending

    We assess affordability to ensure you can repay without financial strain.

    Eligibility

    To qualify for EasyPay, you must meet the following requirements.

    Registered Nobles Cooperative member
    Regular verifiable salary income
    Successful affordability assessment
    Successful credit assessment
    Employer verification where applicable

    How It Works

    Getting started with EasyPay is simple. Follow these five steps.

    1
    Become a Member

    Join Nobles Cooperative with your ₦1,000 lifetime share.

    2
    Submit Application

    Apply for EasyPay through the Nobles Vault app or at our Awka office.

    3
    Provide Documents

    Submit salary slips, bank statements, and verification documents.

    4
    Verification & Appraisal

    Complete verification and affordability assessment.

    5
    Receive Approval

    If eligible, your loan is approved and disbursed.

    Loan Structure

    EasyPay is structured to align with your salary cycle and income.

    6 Months Repayment

    Monthly repayments
    Structured payment schedule
    Aligned with salary cycle
    Predictable monthly instalments

    Affordability-Based

    Maximum eligibility determined by approved affordability assessment
    Processing and insurance charges apply
    Charges in line with approved product policy
    Responsible lending framework

    Processing and insurance charges apply in accordance with the approved product policy. All terms are confirmed during cooperative appraisal.

    Required Documents

    Prepare these documents before starting your application.

    BVN and NIN
    Passport photograph
    Recent salary slips
    Recent bank statements
    Employer verification documents where required
    Additional documents requested during appraisal

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions about EasyPay Salary Loan.

    EasyPay is available to registered Nobles Cooperative members with regular, verifiable salary income. You must also pass the affordability and credit assessment.

    The maximum eligibility is determined by your approved affordability assessment. This ensures you can comfortably manage monthly repayments without financial strain.

    EasyPay is specifically designed for salary earners with regular, verifiable employment income. Self-employed members may be eligible for other Nobles loan products.

    Please inform our team immediately if you change employment. We'll work with you to ensure your repayment plan remains affordable and sustainable.

    Approval typically takes 2–5 working days depending on the completeness of your documents and employer verification.
