    Household Support Loan

    Support Your Home with Confidence.

    KitchenSaver helps eligible members finance essential household food and grocery purchases through a simple and responsible repayment plan.

    Household Financing

    What is KitchenSaver?

    KitchenSaver is a household support loan designed to help members purchase food and other essential household items without placing unnecessary pressure on their monthly finances.

    Key Features

    KitchenSaver is built to support your family's essential needs with simplicity and responsibility.

    Essential Household Purchases

    Designed specifically for food, groceries and other essential household items.

    Responsible Lending

    We assess affordability to ensure you can manage repayments without financial strain.

    Structured Repayment

    A clear, manageable repayment plan that fits your household budget.

    Simple Application

    Straightforward application process with minimal paperwork.

    Who It Is For

    KitchenSaver is designed for members managing household expenses responsibly.

    Families
    Salary Earners
    Small Business Owners
    Members Managing Household Expenses

    How It Works

    Getting started with KitchenSaver is simple. Follow these five steps.

    1
    Become a Member

    Join Nobles Cooperative with your ₦1,000 lifetime share.

    2
    Apply for KitchenSaver

    Submit your application through the Nobles Vault app or at our Awka office.

    3
    Submit Documents

    Provide BVN, NIN, and proof of income or repayment capacity.

    4
    Verification & Appraisal

    Complete verification and affordability assessment.

    5
    Receive Approval & Repay

    If eligible, your loan is approved and you repay according to your agreed schedule.

    Responsible Borrowing

    KitchenSaver is designed to support your household, not strain it.

    Borrow Responsibly

    • Borrow only what you can comfortably repay. Your financial wellbeing is our priority.
    • KitchenSaver is intended for genuine household needs — food, groceries and other essential items that support your family's welfare.
    • Responsible financial planning helps you avoid unnecessary debt and maintain peace of mind.

    Required Documents

    Prepare these documents before starting your application.

    BVN and NIN
    Passport photograph
    Proof of income or repayment capacity
    Additional documents requested during appraisal
