Responsible credit

Kitchen Saver

A responsible-credit pathway for members who first confirm the current Kitchen Saver structure, subject to cooperative appraisal.

Who it is for

members who first confirm the current Kitchen Saver structure.

The need it addresses

a product need that requires current written clarification.

Staff must confirm whether the current structure is credit, savings or a combined arrangement before enrolment.

How appraisal works

  1. 1

    Explain the intended use and your expected cash flow.

  2. 2

    Complete the product-specific cooperative appraisal.

  3. 3

    Review principal, interest, charges, savings requirements and every due date separately.

  4. 4

    Accept only if the full repayment plan remains affordable.

Responsible process

Product-specific terms

Nobles loan products do not use one universal rate or formula.

Clear confirmation

Current terms are confirmed during cooperative appraisal.

Human support

Members can receive assistance through WhatsApp or at the Awka office.

Borrow responsibly

Borrow only for a clear need and only when repayments remain affordable after essential household and business costs. Final approval follows cooperative appraisal.
