    Business Financing

    What is Empower Loan?

    Empower Loan is a business financing solution designed to help qualified entrepreneurs acquire productive assets or fund working capital needs through responsible lending.

    Loan Options

    Choose the financing option that best fits your business needs.

    Empower Asset Plan

    Nobles finances approved productive assets while the member contributes the required equity. Perfect for equipment, machinery, and long-term business investments.

    Empower Working Capital Plan

    Financing for eligible businesses to purchase inventory or meet approved operating needs. Ideal for stock procurement and seasonal business demands.

    Key Features

    Empower Loan is built for serious business growth with responsible lending at its core.

    Short Repayment Periods

    Structured repayment terms designed to match your business cash flow and asset life.

    Responsible Credit Assessment

    We evaluate affordability to ensure you can repay without financial strain.

    Eligibility

    To qualify for Empower Loan, you must meet the following requirements.

    Registered Nobles Cooperative member
    Established business
    Successful credit and affordability assessment
    Business verification
    Approved collateral or security requirements where applicable

    How It Works

    Getting started with Empower Loan is simple. Follow these five steps.

    1
    Become a Member

    Join Nobles Cooperative with your ₦1,000 lifetime share.

    2
    Select Your Option

    Choose between Empower Asset Plan or Empower Working Capital Plan.

    3
    Submit Documents

    Provide BVN, NIN, business verification and financial records.

    4
    Complete Appraisal

    Undergo verification and credit assessment.

    5
    Receive Approval

    If eligible, your loan is approved and disbursed.

    Required Documents

    Prepare these documents before starting your application.

    BVN and NIN
    Passport photograph
    Business verification documents
    Business financial records where required
    Collateral or security documents where applicable
    Additional documents requested during appraisal

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions about Empower Loan.

    The Empower Asset Plan finances productive assets like equipment and machinery with member equity contribution. The Empower Working Capital Plan provides financing for inventory and operating needs.

    The maximum facility is ₦2,000,000, subject to appraisal and approval. The final amount depends on your business assessment, affordability, and security.

    Collateral or security requirements may apply depending on the loan amount and risk assessment. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements during appraisal.

    Existing loan holders in good standing may be eligible. Final approval depends on the credit assessment and your repayment capacity.

    Approval typically takes 3–7 working days depending on the completeness of your documents and the complexity of the assessment.
