Savings product

Monthly Savings Challenge

A savings-discipline challenge presented separately from the tenor-based Future Fund product.

Open Nobles VaultAsk staff on WhatsApp

Who it is for

Members who want a recurring monthly prompt to strengthen their savings habit.

The problem it addresses

A savings goal can lose momentum when there is no regular rhythm for setting money aside.

How it works

  1. 1

    Choose the savings goal you want the challenge to support.

  2. 2

    Ask staff for the currently approved challenge duration and contribution rules.

  3. 3

    Enrol through the approved Nobles channel.

  4. 4

    Make contributions according to the confirmed challenge rules.

Benefits

Practical benefit

Provides a clear monthly savings focus.

Practical benefit

Is explicitly separate from Future Fund and its tenor-based interest schedule.

Practical benefit

Offers staff assistance through WhatsApp and the Awka office.

How to enrol

  1. 1

    Download or open Nobles Vault and register as directed.

  2. 2

    Ask staff to confirm every term marked for confirmation on this page.

  3. 3

    Complete assisted office enrolment where the product requires it.

Before you commit

Choose a monthly amount that is realistic after essential expenses. The challenge does not use the Future Fund tenor schedule unless an approved product source later states otherwise.
