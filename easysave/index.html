Savings product

EasySave

A Nobles savings option for members who want practical support to build a consistent savings habit.

Open Nobles VaultAsk staff on WhatsApp

Who it is for

Members who want to begin or strengthen a personal savings routine with support from Nobles Cooperative.

The problem it addresses

It can be difficult to separate money for future needs when household and business demands compete for the same cash.

How it works

  1. 1

    Choose EasySave as the savings option you want to discuss.

  2. 2

    Ask staff to explain the currently approved contribution and access rules.

  3. 3

    Enrol through Nobles Vault or complete assisted enrolment where required.

  4. 4

    Follow the agreed savings plan and review progress in the approved channel.

Benefits

Practical benefit

A named savings pathway inside the Nobles product range.

Practical benefit

Access to human support through WhatsApp and the Awka office.

Practical benefit

A structured next step for building savings discipline.

How to enrol

  1. 1

    Download or open Nobles Vault and register as directed.

  2. 2

    Ask staff to confirm every term marked for confirmation on this page.

  3. 3

    Complete assisted office enrolment where the product requires it.

Before you commit

Confirm the contribution, access and withdrawal conditions before enrolling. Choose an amount that leaves enough money for essential household and business needs.
