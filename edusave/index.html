    Education Savings

    What is EduSave?

    EduSave is Nobles Cooperative's planned school-expense account designed to help parents, guardians, teachers and families prepare gradually for school fees and other education costs. Members can make affordable weekly or monthly contributions toward fees, books, uniforms, examinations, transport and other approved school expenses.

    Do not wait until school resumes before looking for school fees. With Nobles EduSave, you can contribute a manageable amount every week or month and prepare ahead for your child's education expenses.

    Key Benefits

    EduSave helps you protect your family from school-fee pressure and last-minute borrowing.

    Prevent Last-Minute Borrowing

    Save gradually for school fees so you never have to borrow urgently when school resumes.

    Reduce School Resumption Pressure

    Walk into every school term with confidence knowing your child's fees are already planned for.

    Protect Business Capital

    Keep your business money separate from school expenses. No more emergency withdrawals from working capital.

    Keep School Money Separate

    A dedicated account ensures education funds are never mixed with everyday spending.

    Build Disciplined Habits

    Develop a consistent weekly or monthly contribution habit that grows your education fund steadily.

    Reduce Interruption of Education

    Ensure your children's schooling is never interrupted by delayed fee payments.

    Withdrawal Policy

    EduSave is designed to encourage disciplined savings for education expenses.

    Planned Withdrawals

    • One free withdrawal is allowed per calendar quarter.
    • Additional withdrawals within the same quarter will result in forfeiture of the expected savings return, in line with the approved product policy.
    • Plan ahead — use your quarterly withdrawal strategically for school resumption expenses.
