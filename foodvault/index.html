    Seasonal Food Savings

    Save Today. Celebrate Tomorrow.

    Food Vault helps you spread the cost of festive food shopping by saving consistently throughout the year, making December celebrations easier and stress-free.

    Join Food Vault Download Nobles Vault

    Seasonal Programme

    What is Food Vault?

    Food Vault is Nobles Cooperative's seasonal savings programme that allows members to save from February to November and receive their food package or approved cash option in December.

    Key Benefits

    Food Vault takes the stress out of festive food shopping.

    Eliminates Festive Financial Pressure

    Save gradually so December spending doesn't strain your monthly budget.

    Encourages Disciplined Seasonal Saving

    Build a consistent saving habit throughout the year with a clear goal.

    Convenient Digital Contributions

    Save anytime, anywhere through the Nobles Vault app.

    Flexible Payout Options

    Choose between a food package or the approved Cash Anchor option.

    How Food Vault Works

    Save through the year, celebrate in December. It's that simple.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions about Food Vault.

    Food Vault enrolment opens in February each year and runs through November. Join early to get the most benefit from the programme.

    Each plan includes a curated selection of festive food items. Package contents vary by plan — Big Madam Plan offers the most comprehensive selection.

    Plan changes may be permitted during the savings period, subject to programme terms. Please contact staff for assistance.

    Consistent contributions are encouraged to ensure you receive your full package in December. If you miss a contribution, speak with staff to explore options.

    Food packages are delivered in December, just in time for the festive season. You'll be notified of collection or delivery arrangements.
