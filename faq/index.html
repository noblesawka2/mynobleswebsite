    Help & Support

    Frequently Asked Questions.

    Find quick answers to common questions about membership, savings, loans, the Nobles Vault App and our services.

    Contact Support Become a Member

    To become a member, simply download the Nobles Vault app from the Google Play Store or visit our office at No. 331B Ziks Avenue (MTN Office), Amaenyi, Awka, Anambra State. The membership fee is a one-time ₦1,000 lifetime share. Our team will guide you through the registration process.

    Membership

    Nobles Cooperative is open to women, families, salary earners, small business owners and entrepreneurs in South-East Nigeria. We welcome anyone who is committed to building financial stability through disciplined savings and responsible financial practices.

    Membership

    Your membership ID is assigned during registration. You can find it in the Nobles Vault app under your profile, or on your membership card. If you need assistance, contact our support team.

    Membership

    We offer several savings products including EasySave (daily/weekly savings), Future Fund (goal-based savings with 3–12 month tenors), Gold Vault (premium savings with 12-month tenor and up to 14.4% p.a. returns), EduSave (planned school-expense account), Group Savings (rotational savings), Monthly Savings Challenge (habit-building), and Food Vault (seasonal food savings programme).

    Savings

    Withdrawal and early-access rules vary by product. Some products allow free withdrawals (e.g., EduSave allows one free withdrawal per quarter). For Gold Vault and Future Fund, early withdrawal may be subject to penalties or loss of expected returns. Please confirm the specific terms with Nobles staff before making a commitment.

    Savings

    EduSave covers school fees, books and learning materials, uniforms and shoes, examination fees, transportation, boarding, and other approved education expenses. It's designed to help families prepare gradually for school costs without last-minute borrowing.

    Savings

    Loan applications are processed through the Nobles Vault app. You'll need to be an active member with a savings history. Our team will guide you through the credit assessment and vetting process. Different loan products (MarketLift, MarketPower, Unity Loan, EasyPay, Empower Loan, Kitchen Saver) have different eligibility requirements.

    Loans

    Interest rates and charges vary by product and are confirmed during the cooperative appraisal process. MarketLift and MarketPower have upfront deductions, while other products have structured repayment plans. We recommend speaking with staff to understand the current approved terms for each loan product.

    Loans

    Unity Loan is a group-based business loan where members support one another through shared responsibility, weekly meetings and disciplined repayments. A minimum of 5 registered members is required to form an approved group. The group must meet regularly and contribute to a group purse. 20% compulsory savings is required before disbursement.

    Loans

    Nobles Vault App

    You can reset your password using the "Forgot Password" option on the app login screen. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email or phone number. If you continue to experience issues, contact our support team for assistance.

    Nobles Vault App

    If you're experiencing issues with the app, first try updating to the latest version. If the problem persists, clear your app cache or reinstall the app. For ongoing issues, contact our support team on WhatsApp at +234 915 114 2355 or visit our Awka office for assistance.

    Nobles Vault App

    Contributions can be made through the Nobles Vault app using your bank account or mobile wallet. You can also make cash contributions at our Awka office. The app provides clear instructions for each payment method.

    Payments & Withdrawals

    Withdrawal processing times vary depending on the product and the withdrawal method. Standard withdrawals are typically processed within 24–48 hours. For large amounts or special requests, additional verification may be required.
