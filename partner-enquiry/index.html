    What to Include in Your Enquiry

    Share only the information needed to start a meaningful conversation.

    Your Name & Organisation — Tell us who you are and who you represent.
    Contact Details — Email, phone number, and preferred contact method.
    Partnership Interest — What type of partnership are you exploring (CSR, sponsorship, grant, etc.)?
    Concise Proposal Summary — Briefly outline your partnership idea or proposal.

    Security note: Do not send banking credentials, member data, or confidential records in your initial enquiry. We'll request any additional information through secure channels.
